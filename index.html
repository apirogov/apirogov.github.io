My name is Anton Pirogov and this is my homepage.

I have studied computer science and bioinformatics and completed my M.Sc. at the University of Lübeck and am currently pursuing a PhD at the RWTH Aachen University.

I enjoy functional programming and other programming languages which broaden my horizon and extend my toolbox with new useful concepts and ideas. I am interested in methods to write clean, correct and robust software.

I am also curious about discrete math, logic, automata, formal systems, type theory, category theory and other abstract but beautiful topics.

In my free time I sometimes like to tinker on hobby projects, learn new things, read books (mostly non-fiction or hard sci-fi), listen to music and make music myself (drums, also piano).
